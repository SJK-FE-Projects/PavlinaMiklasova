Pavlina Miklasova

Who I am

I am a founder and art director of studio Plove®. As one of the few fashion designers, I focus on all-Czech swimwear accenting innovative attitude and functionality. My collections are preceded by material experiments; by examining material in different contexts I try to define and reappraise both function and form of the product given. I have a special passion for working with knitted & elastic materials. I graduated in 2017 from the Academy of Art, Architecture, and Design in Prague, Czech Republic - Fashion and Shoe Design Studio of Liběna Rochová.
